Life at Obsidian

Serious work.
Extraordinary care.

We ask a great deal of the people who work here. In return, we've built a place where excellence is rewarded, differences are an asset, and careers span decades.

What we believe

Four principles, non-negotiable.

Client first, always

Every decision starts with the people who trust us with their capital. It has for 150 years.

Excellence is the floor

From the trading floor to the dining floor, "good enough" isn't in our vocabulary.

Integrity without audience

We do the right thing when no one is watching — because in banking, someone always is.

One firm

Engineers, traders, cleaners, counsel: one payroll, one standard of respect, one team.

Benefits

Taken care of,
so you can take care.

Top-of-market pay

How hiring works

Five steps.
No mystery.

We tell you the process up front and keep you informed at every stage. Most candidates complete it within four weeks.

1

Apply

Send your application through the role page. A human reads it — every one — within ten business days.

2

Introductory call

Thirty minutes with a recruiter. We tell you about the team and comp range; you tell us what you're looking for.

3

Craft interview

A deep conversation about your work — technical interviews for technical roles, practical scenarios for everything else. No trick questions.

4

Meet the team

Spend time with the people you'd actually work with, including a session where you interview us.

5

Offer

A clear, complete offer with no exploding deadlines. Take the time you need to decide well.
